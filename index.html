About

Us

Established in the Mysore Road with finest ambiance of greenery and world-class infrastructure, far from the hustle-bustle of the city, the Campus provides the experience of the traditional as well as world-class facilities with department-specific buildings, libraries, research facilities and residences. Kengeri Campus brings the business and technology community together to develop transformative ideas and pioneering leaders for this digital era.
